Our Story

Built Around Indian Rug Craftsmanship

Artchemy Nexus was founded to bring structure and reliability to sourcing rugs from India. India's rug industry is supported by generations of skilled artisans and specialized weaving units across the country. We work closely with these partners, coordinating development, production, and export so our international clients can access Indian craftsmanship with clarity and confidence.

The Idea Behind Artchemy Nexus

India has long been one of the world's most important centers for handmade rug production. Across different regions, skilled weaving units produce hand-knotted and hand-tufted rugs using techniques refined over generations. For international clients, however, navigating this ecosystem can be complex. Production capabilities vary between regions, communication gaps can occur, and managing quality, timelines, and export logistics often requires local coordination. Artchemy Nexus was established to simplify this process.
