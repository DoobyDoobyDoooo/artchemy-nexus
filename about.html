About Artchemy Nexus

A quiet bridge between Indian craft and global luxury interiors.

Our Story

Built for buyers who see sourcing as a creative responsibility.

Artchemy Nexus was created to serve international retailers, interior designers, boutique stores, hospitality projects, and private-label brands seeking handmade rugs from India with clarity and refinement.

India has one of the world’s richest weaving ecosystems. Yet global buyers often need more than manufacturing access. They need translation: between design intent and craft process, between price architecture and quality, between sampling and reliable export.

Our role is to make that journey feel considered, calm, and commercially intelligent.

Mission

To connect India’s handmade rug heritage with global buyers through disciplined sourcing and refined development.

Vision

To become the most trusted quiet partner for luxury rug sourcing from India.

Global Outlook

Rooted in India. Built for international expectations.

Our work is shaped by the realities of export buyers: consistency, documentation, communication, timelines, and the subtle details that make a rug feel right in a finished space.

How We Think

Every collection moves through a quiet sequence of decisions.

01

Understand the Buyer

We begin with market position, customer profile, design direction, pricing, quantities, and delivery realities.

02

Translate the Brief

Design references become material choices, construction methods, colour direction, texture, pile, and finish.

03

Develop With Makers

Sampling and production are aligned with the right craft clusters, capabilities, and quality expectations.

04

Control the Details

Inspection, packaging, documentation, and export coordination help the final product arrive with confidence.
