The Idea Behind Artchemy Nexus

Artchemy Nexus was founded to bring structure and reliability to sourcing rugs from India. India's rug industry is supported by generations of skilled artisans. We work closely with these partners, coordinating development, production, and export so our international clients can access Indian craftsmanship with clarity and confidence.

How We Work

We work directly with trusted weaving units. Managing the development, production, and export stages, we provide our clients with a structured and reliable way to source rugs from India.
